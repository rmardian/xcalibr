JUMP Assembly Tool

Domesticate Part Simulate Assembly Visualize Construct Automate Design Guidelines

The software that makes your JUMP easier. Trust me.


Currently only supports FASTA format (GENBANK is coming soon). You'll need to annotate your plasmid maps using another software. Domesticating parts by removing any internal restriction site (i.e. BsaI and BsmBI sites) will also be supported in the future version.

Use "Domesticate Parts" to create level-0 parts out of template plasmids, "Simulate Assembly" to simulate JUMP from an assembly plan and generate the plasmid maps in-bulk in FASTA format, or "Automate Assembly" to perform combinatorial assembly out of available parts and a pre-determined design.
